About

In many businesses, HR operations and employee management are still conducted manually, leading to inefficiencies and errors. Our GYM Employee Management and HR Operations System is designed to address these issues by providing an integrated solution that streamlines HR tasks and enhances the management of employee data. This project is inspired by the need to modernize and automate these processes, offering a more efficient and reliable system for businesses. It's also a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
